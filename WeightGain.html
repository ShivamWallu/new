Tips for Healthy Weight Gain

1 Consume a Calorie Surplus: Aim for 300-500 extra calories per day to promote weight gain.
2 Prioritize Protein: Include lean meats, poultry, fish, tofu, and dairy for muscle growth.
3 Healthy Fats: Incorporate avocados, nuts, seeds, and olive oil for added calories and nutrients.
4 Complex Carbs: Opt for whole grains like brown rice, quinoa, and whole-grain bread.
5 Frequent Meals: Eat 5-6 smaller meals throughout the day to increase calorie intake.
6 Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water to support overall health during weight gain.
7 Strength Training: Include resistance exercises to build muscle mass.
8 Monitor Progress: Keep track of your weight gain and adjust your diet accordingly.

Remember to consult a healthcare professional or a registered dietitian before making any significant changes to your diet.
